# Product Strategy Next

## What Competitors Do

Most Polymarket-adjacent tools optimize for speed and power users:

- Trading terminals focus on faster execution, dense market tables, wallet workflows, and advanced filters.
- Copy-style tools focus on following public participants and portfolio behavior.
- Analytics dashboards focus on market data, volume, rankings, and alerts.
- Social channels focus on individual market calls or news-like updates.

These tools are useful for experienced users, but they often assume the user already knows what to look for.

## What They Miss

New and casual users still need a simple companion that answers:

- What matters today?
- Why is this market worth attention?
- What changed?
- What should I watch next?
- How does this market resolve?

The missing product is not another terminal. The missing product is a daily interpretation layer.

## What Users Probably Need

Users need a fast daily ritual:

1. Open PulseMarket AI.
2. See the short briefing.
3. Understand the most notable markets.
4. Notice where public attention is moving.
5. Open Polymarket only when they want to inspect the source market.

They do not need raw wallet lists, unexplained scores, or a screen full of metrics before they understand the story.

## What PulseMarket AI Should Become

PulseMarket AI should become the **Daily Market Intelligence Companion for Polymarket**.

Core promise:

> Understand what matters on Polymarket today.

RU:

> Пойми, что сегодня важно на Polymarket.

PulseMarket AI should feel like a morning briefing: curated, short, calm, and useful.

## Proposed Unique Product Angle

Competitors help users trade faster.

PulseMarket AI helps users understand markets faster.

The product should prioritize meaning before metrics:

- Morning Briefing instead of raw market feeds.
- Attention Shift instead of raw wallet lists.
- Market Mood instead of unexplained activity numbers.
- Simple Read instead of technical market descriptions.
- Personal Topics instead of generic discovery for everyone.

## Features To Build Now

### Morning Briefing

Upgrade Today's Pulse into the daily home screen:

- 1 main market
- 2 secondary markets
- 1 attention shift
- short why people care
- clear next action

### Market Mood

Add a human-readable label to every important market:

- Quiet
- Active
- Heating up
- Volatile
- Ending soon

RU:

- Тихо
- Активно
- Разогревается
- Волатильно
- Скоро завершение

### Simple Read

Every market should answer:

- What this market asks
- Why people care
- What to watch
- Resolution note

### Attention Shift

Activity Radar should explain where attention is moving, not lead with wallets.

### Share Layer

Make the daily briefing and market cards easy to forward without hype language.

## Features To Avoid

- Trading
- Copy trading
- Deposits
- Wallet connection
- Balances
- Order placement
- Buy/sell advice
- Profit promises
- Raw wallet feeds as the main product
- Dense exchange-like dashboards

## Success Metrics

- Daily Mini App opens
- `/today` usage
- Search queries
- Market opens
- Saved markets
- Shared market summaries
- Feedback submissions
- Channel digest engagement
- Returning users after 1 day and 7 days

## Product North Star

A user should understand the day's Polymarket picture in under 30 seconds.
