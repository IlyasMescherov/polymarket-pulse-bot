# PulseMarket AI Avatar Guide

## Requirements

- 1024x1024 PNG.
- Must read clearly inside a small Telegram circle.
- No tiny text.
- No official Polymarket logo.
- Dark fintech style.
- Blue and green accents.
- Minimal premium brand mark.
- Strong silhouette that still works at 40x40 px.

## Direction

The avatar should feel like a serious market-discovery product, not a trading or crypto-shilling logo. Keep it simple, high-contrast, and recognizable.

## Prompt Option 1

```text
PM monogram, premium fintech app icon, dark navy background, blue and green accent, minimal geometric mark, high contrast, clean vector-like style, no small text, no official Polymarket logo, 1024x1024
```

abstract signal mark for a market discovery assistant, no letters, human-crafted premium fintech style, dark background, blue green glow accent, simple iconic silhouette, not AI generic, no chart cliché, no official Polymarket logo, 1024x1024

```text
bold simple app icon for probability and market discovery, strong silhouette, minimal premium fintech brand, dark navy, blue and green accents, no tiny text, no candlestick cliché, no official Polymarket logo, 1024x1024
```
