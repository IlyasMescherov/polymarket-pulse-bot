# Morning Briefing

Morning Briefing is the next product layer for PulseMarket AI.

It turns Today's Pulse into a daily companion experience:

> Understand what matters on Polymarket today.

RU:

> Пойми, что сегодня важно на Polymarket.

## Experience

The user opens PulseMarket AI and immediately sees:

- 1 main story
- 2 secondary stories
- 1 attention shift
- Market Mood labels
- short why people care
- a clear next action

The goal is not to show every market. The goal is to help the user understand the day quickly.

## Card Structure

Each market card should answer:

1. What is the market?
2. Why do people care?
3. What should I watch?
4. Where can I inspect the source market?

## Copy Rules

Prefer:

- Attention is rising around this market.
- People are watching this because activity increased.
- Probability moved today.
- The market is close to resolution.

Avoid:

- Long explanations before the user understands the market.
- Raw data without meaning.
- Hype language.
- Directional trading language.

## Safety Scope

Morning Briefing is research-only. It does not execute trades, connect wallets, manage funds, or provide financial advice.
