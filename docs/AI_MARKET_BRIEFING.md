# AI Market Briefing

PulseMarket AI is evolving into a daily market intelligence companion for Polymarket.

The briefing experience should answer three questions quickly:

1. What matters today?
2. Why are people watching it?
3. What should I inspect next?

## Today’s Narrative

The Mini App includes a short narrative block:

```text
What markets are reacting to today.
```

It summarizes the dominant categories and topic movement in one calm sentence.

## What Changed

The briefing includes a compact habit layer:

```text
What changed
```

Examples:

- Attention moved toward politics markets.
- Several markets are close to resolution.
- Crypto is noticeable in today’s briefing.

## Market Context

Each market can include:

- Why people care
- Simple Read
- What to watch
- Market Mood reasoning

Only one or two short lines should appear in the main UI. Longer context belongs behind Explain.

## Probability Language

PulseMarket AI shows human probability interpretation before raw numbers when useful:

- Very low probability
- Possible
- Likely
- Highly likely

Russian:

- Очень низкая вероятность
- Возможно
- Вероятно
- Очень вероятно

The number remains visible as secondary context.

## Safety

The briefing is research-only.

It does not tell users what decision to make, does not execute actions, and does not promise outcomes.
